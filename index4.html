Boston Bruins

The Boston Bruins were founded in 1924 making them the third oldest team in the NHL(National Hockey League). The Bruins play in the TD Garden Arena just like the Celtics. The Bruins have also been a very successful New England team with their 6 Stanley Cups. The Bruins most recent championship came in the 2010-11 season when goalie Tim Thomas played lights out in the playoffs.
