Boston Celtics

The Boston Celtics were founded in 1946 as one of the first NBA teams. The Celtics have the
most championships in the NBA with 17. 13 out of the Celtics 17 championships came before 1980
and modern basketball. The Boston Celtics struggled to compete for most of the 90's and early to mid 2000's.
Celtics General Manager Danny Ainge revived the Celtics franchise in 2008 when he paired up
3 superstars and brought a championship back to Boston. The Celtics have been competing
since their resurgence. Bill Russell(played in the 50's and 60's) and Larry Bird
(played in the late 70', all the 80's, and early 90's) and two of the best players all time
and brought many championships to Boston.
