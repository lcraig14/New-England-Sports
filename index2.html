New England Patriots

The New England Patriots were founded in 1959 as a member of the AFL(American Football League).
The Patriots joined the NFL in 1970 when the NFL and AFL merged. New England is located in Foxborough,
Massachusetts. The Patriots have only had two different stadiums since joining the NFL, Foxboro Stadium(1971-2001)
and Gillette Stadium(2002- current). The Patriots have been dominating for the last two decades with
Tom Brady and coach Bill Belichick leading the team. The pair have won all 5 of the Patriots Super Bowls
Tom Brady and Bill Belichick are two of the best to ever in the NFL.
