Boston Red Sox

The Boston Red Sox are the oldest New England sports team since they were founded
in 1901. The Red Sox have played at Fenway Park since 1912 and is one of the
most historic and recognizable stadiums in baseball. The Red Sox have won 8 MLB
World Series. Greats like Babe Roth and Ted Williams are two of the biggest contributors
to making the Red Sox so historic. They both had unbelievable careers.
